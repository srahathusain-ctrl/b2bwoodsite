# Letter of Credit (LC) Documentation Checklist
### Steel Wood Industries FZCO — Export Operations

## Pre-Shipment Documents
- [ ] Commercial Invoice (3 originals)
- [ ] Packing List
- [ ] Bill of Lading / Airway Bill
- [ ] Certificate of Origin (UAE Chamber of Commerce)
- [ ] FSC Certificate (valid & current)
- [ ] Test/Quality Certificate
- [ ] Phytosanitary Certificate (if applicable)

## Financial Documents
- [ ] LC copy reviewed vs. Proforma Invoice
- [ ] Discrepancies flagged to bank within 5 days
- [ ] LC expiry date confirmed
- [ ] Latest shipment date confirmed
- [ ] Partial shipment terms noted

## Post-Shipment
- [ ] Documents presented to bank within LC validity
- [ ] Swift copy of LC negotiation obtained
- [ ] Customer advised of document dispatch
